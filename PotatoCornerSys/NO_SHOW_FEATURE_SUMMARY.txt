========================================
NO SHOW FEATURE - IMPLEMENTATION SUMMARY
========================================

WHAT WAS ADDED:
- Automatic "No Show" status for walk-in orders not picked up within 60 minutes
- Stock refund system for no-show orders
- Orange color coding for no-show orders
- Revenue tracking (no-show orders count as revenue)

========================================
HOW IT WORKS
========================================

WALK-IN ORDER TIMELINE:

0 min:  Customer places walk-in order → PENDING
5 min:  Admin confirms → CONFIRMED
        (Customer has 60 minutes to pick up)

Within 60 min: Customer arrives → Admin marks PICKED UP ✓

After 60 min:  No customer → Auto changes to NO SHOW ⚠
               - Stock automatically refunded
               - Money stays with business
               - Customer sees "No Show" in profile

========================================
BUSINESS LOGIC
========================================

When order becomes "No Show":

✓ MONEY: Stays with business (NO refund to customer)
✓ STOCK: Automatically refunded (available to sell again)
✓ REVENUE: Counts in total revenue
✓ CUSTOMER: Sees "No Show - Not Picked Up" status
✓ ADMIN: Sees orange "No Show" badge

This protects your business from:
- Customers who order but don't show up
- Wasted preparation time
- Held inventory that could be sold

========================================
STOCK REFUND DETAILS
========================================

When a walk-in order hits 60 minutes:

1. System finds all "Confirmed" walk-in orders older than 60 min
2. For each order:
   - Gets all order items
   - Refunds product size stock (1:1 ratio)
   - Refunds flavor stock (1:10 ratio)
3. Changes order status to "No Show"
4. Stock is back in inventory for new orders

Example:
- Order: 3 Large Fries with Cheese
- After 60 min no-show:
  - Large size stock: +3 units
  - Cheese flavor stock: +1 unit (3÷10 = 0.3, rounded up to 1)

========================================
CUSTOMER EXPERIENCE
========================================

In Profile.aspx, customer sees:

Status Badge: "No Show - Not Picked Up" (Orange)
Message: Order was not picked up within the time limit
Actions: Can only "Reorder" (cannot cancel)
Refund: NONE - Money is forfeited

This teaches customers to:
- Pick up orders on time
- Cancel if they can't make it
- Be responsible with their orders

========================================
ADMIN EXPERIENCE
========================================

In Sales.aspx, admin sees:

Status Badge: "No Show" (Orange background)
Row Style: Orange tinted (different from cancelled)
Revenue: INCLUDED in total revenue
Action: No buttons (order is final)

Benefits:
- Easy to spot no-show customers
- Track which customers frequently don't show
- Revenue accurately reflects money received
- Stock automatically available for resale

========================================
TIMING BREAKDOWN
========================================

0-10 minutes:
- Customer can cancel (gets refund)
- Admin can confirm

10-60 minutes:
- Customer CANNOT cancel
- Admin waiting for pickup
- Order stays "Confirmed"

60+ minutes:
- Auto-changes to "No Show"
- Stock refunded automatically
- Money kept by business
- Customer cannot cancel

========================================
DIFFERENCES FROM CANCELLED
========================================

CANCELLED:
- Customer initiated (within 10 min)
- Money refunded to customer
- Stock refunded
- NOT counted in revenue
- Gray/pale appearance

NO SHOW:
- System initiated (after 60 min)
- Money kept by business
- Stock refunded
- COUNTED in revenue
- Orange appearance

========================================
CONFIGURATION
========================================

To change the no-show timeout:

In Sales.aspx.cs, find this line:
AND DATEDIFF(MINUTE, OrderDate, GETDATE()) >= 60

Change 60 to your desired minutes:
- 30 = 30 minutes
- 45 = 45 minutes
- 90 = 90 minutes

Recommended: 60 minutes (1 hour)
